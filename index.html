Video Audio Synchronizer (Client-Side)

Upload your video and audio files, and this app will stretch/compress the video to match the audio duration using FFmpeg.wasm in your browser.

Important Notes:

            
    
                
  • Processing happens entirely in your browser. No files are uploaded to a server.
    • 
                
  • **Large files may cause the browser tab to crash due to memory limitations.** Try with smaller files if you encounter issues.
    • 
                
  • Compatibility: Uses WebAssembly (WASM), which works on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
    • 
                
  • Initial load time: FFmpeg.wasm library needs to be downloaded the first time (around 20MB).
