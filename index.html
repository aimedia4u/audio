Dynamic Audio Visualizer

Upload an audio file (e.g., MP3, WAV, OGG) to see its waveform and spectrogram.

This tool processes audio in your browser. For video files, specialized tools like `ffmpeg.wasm` are needed for in-browser audio extraction, which is beyond the scope of this basic example.
